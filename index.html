Field Day 2026

Curtis Memorial Park, Stafford VA  ·  June 27–28, 2026

Welcome to the Rappahannock Valley Amateur Radio Club's Field Day information hub. Field Day is amateur radio's largest annual operating event — a 24-hour public demonstration of emergency preparedness and community service, held simultaneously by clubs across North America.

Visiting us at the park? Stop by our Public Information Table to learn about amateur radio, pick up free brochures, and try your hand at making a contact at our GOTA (Get On The Air) station — no license required.

What Is Field Day?

Every year on the fourth weekend of June, amateur radio operators set up portable stations in parks, parking lots, and emergency operations centers — running entirely on battery or generator power, with no connections to the commercial grid. The goal is to practice the skills that make ham radio invaluable when other communications fail.

RVARC has participated in Field Day for decades. This year we're operating from Curtis Memorial Park in Stafford, Virginia, with stations on multiple bands and modes.
